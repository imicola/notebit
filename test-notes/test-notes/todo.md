# Welcome to Notebit!

This is your new **Local-First** Markdown note-taking application.

## Features

- **Pure Editor**: Distraction-free writing environment
- **Live Preview**: See your markdown rendered in real-time
- **Dark Theme**: Easy on the eyes
- **Local Storage**: Your notes stay on your machine
